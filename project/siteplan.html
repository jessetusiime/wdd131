Travel Uganda - Site Plan

1. Site Name

Travel Uganda

2. Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to showcase Uganda as a top travel destination by highlighting its attractions, culture, food, and travel planning resources. It will demonstrate responsive design, accessibility, best practices and JavaScript interactivity.

3. Scenarios

  • What are the top destinations in Uganda?
  • What traditional foods should I try in Uganda?
  • How can I plan my trip within my budget?
